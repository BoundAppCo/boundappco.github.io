What is bound?

The modern age reminder system

Plan

Tell Bound when your meeting is. Simply set the date, time, and location.

price your time

Bound will reward you or penalize you for attending or missing meetings.

improve

Improve your time management skills and never be late again.

How does Bound work?


Bet your friends

Place a bet as an incentive to get to a meeting on time. It can be for work, friends, etc.


Win cash or lose cash

If you get there on time, you'll win your friend's bet. If you're late, your friend will win yours.
